Local History Night

Event Overview

Date & Time: Thursday, November 7th, 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM

Location: Conference Room, Silent Library

Event Description

Take a journey through time at our Local History Night. This event features a captivating presentation by local historians and authors who will share intriguing stories about the history of our community. Whether you’re a history buff or simply curious about the past, you’ll gain a deeper understanding of the events, people, and places that have shaped our town. The evening will conclude with a Q&A session, offering an opportunity to engage directly with the presenters. This is a must-attend event for anyone interested in the rich heritage of our community.

Special Notes

  • Complimentary copies of selected historical maps and documents will be available.
  • Refreshments provided.

Limited copies of the authors’ books will be available for purchase at the event. Light refreshments will be served.
