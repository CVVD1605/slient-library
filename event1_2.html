Book Club Meetings

Event Overview

Date & Time: Every First Wednesday of the Month, 7:00 PM – 8:30 PM

Location: Reading Room, Silent Library

Event Description

Whether you're a mystery lover, a science fiction fan, or have a soft spot for historical fiction, our monthly Book Club Meetings have something for everyone. Each month, we focus on a different genre, allowing participants to explore diverse narratives and share their thoughts in a friendly, relaxed environment. This is a great opportunity to discover new authors, meet like-minded readers, and deepen your appreciation for various literary genres. All reading levels are welcome, and participation in discussions is encouraged but not required.

Special Notes

  • Upcoming genres: October - Mystery, November - Historical Fiction, December - Science Fiction.
  • Copies of the selected books are available at the library.

Limited copies of the authors’ books will be available for purchase at the event. Light refreshments will be served.
